Our Story

Good taste is important. Honest ingredients, even more so. Which is why, at NIC Ice Creams, we make it our mission to bring you the most delightful of ice creams, ranging from the exotic to the innovative. We have crafted flavours ranging from fruit and dry fruit variations like Tender Coconut, Alphonso Mango, Dry Fruit Overload, Roasted Almonds to international flavors such as Mediterranean Sea Salted Caramel, Madagascar Chocolate, French Vanilla, and those inspired by Indian sweetmeats such as Gulab Jamun, Sheer Khurma, Til Gud, and Gajar Halwa, to name a few..
